
# Parental Leave Analysis Across US Industries 📊

An in-depth Excel and Tableau analysis exploring how parental leave policies vary across different industries in the United States.
[parental-leave-dashboard.pdf](https://github.com/user-attachments/files/27200662/parental-leave-dashboard.pdf)


### Project Overview
This project analyzes parental leave (maternity, paternity, and overall parental leave) across major industries in the US. The goal was to uncover disparities, identify leading and lagging industries, and highlight trends that affect working parents.

---

### Objectives
- Compare parental leave duration and generosity across industries
- Analyze differences between maternity and paternity leave
- Identify industries with the best and worst parental leave policies
- Provide data-driven insights for policymakers, HR professionals, and employees

---

### Key Insights
- Significant variation in parental leave policies exists across industries
- Tech and Finance sectors generally offer more generous parental leave compared to Retail, Hospitality, and Manufacturing
- Paternity leave remains considerably shorter than maternity leave in most industries
- Many industries still fall below the national average or recommended best practices
